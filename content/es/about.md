I'm a Software Engineer who is always looking for new challenges and stays at the bleeding edge of technology. I hold a
Master's Degree in Data Science and Computer Engineering. During my 10-years career, I worked for 2 different areas:
for the Academia, as researcher and PhD student; and, for private companies. Different worlds, but a common ground-
truth of learnt lessons:
- An awful solution to solve a problem today becomes a big headache sooner or later.
- Perfect solutions might exist, time to find them does not.
- Workmates are more than people that drinks coffee with you.
- Time spent on your workmates is an investment, not a waste of time.
- If you plan to reinvent the wheel, get ready to explain why.
My core of knowledge is the design and implementation of systems based on Xilinx's devices, either SoC or FPGA. But
I'm a versatile engineer who strives to get out of his comfort zone, and I also enjoy writing high level software and get
hands on HW laboratory testing.
I have been part of big science projects, such as the European Spallation Source in Sweden, and worked for very
innovative companies such as Clue Technologies and 7Solutions in Spain. So far, I have worked in different sectors, and
I can't say that I have a special preference for a particular type of applications. What I do have is a wiliness to be part of innovative projects that aim to solve the problems of our society.
